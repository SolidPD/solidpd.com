RxWear

Challenge

Laminitis Diagnostic Tool

A veterinary diagnostic device that enables early detection and monitoring of laminitis in horses, improving treatment outcomes.

Challenge

Laminitis detection relied on subjective assessment, often missing early stages when treatment is most effective.

Solution

We developed a portable diagnostic tool using thermal imaging and pressure sensors to provide objective, quantitative measurements.

Results

Adopted by leading veterinary clinics, 85% accuracy in early detection, and significant improvement in treatment success rates.

Veterinary Diagnostic Thermal Imaging
