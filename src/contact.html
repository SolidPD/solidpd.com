Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working with us and our process.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity, but most projects range from 3-12 months. Simple redesigns might take 6-8 weeks, while complex new product development can take 12-18 months from concept to production.

What's included in your design services?

Our comprehensive services include research and strategy, industrial design, mechanical and electrical engineering, prototyping, testing, and manufacturing support. We can handle the entire development process or specific phases.

Do you work with startups?

Absolutely! We love working with startups and have helped many secure funding and launch successful products. We understand the unique challenges startups face and can adapt our approach to fit your budget and timeline.

Can you help with manufacturing?

Yes, we provide comprehensive manufacturing support including supplier selection, production planning, quality assurance, and scaling strategies. We work with trusted manufacturing partners worldwide.

What industries do you serve?

We work across diverse industries including healthcare, consumer electronics, industrial IoT, fitness and sports, automotive, and sustainability. Our team's broad expertise allows us to tackle projects in virtually any sector.
