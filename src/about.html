About SOLID

We're a team of passionate designers and engineers dedicated to bringing innovative hardware products to life.

Our Story

Founded with a mission to bridge the gap between innovative ideas and market-ready products, Solid Product Design has been at the forefront of hardware development for over a decade.

Our team combines deep technical expertise with creative problem-solving to help startups and established companies navigate the complex journey from concept to production.

Based in Philadelphia, we've had the privilege of working with companies across various industries, helping them raise over $100M in funding and bring multiple products to market.
