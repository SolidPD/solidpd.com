Services

Great product design isn't prescriptive; it's about discovery. It comes from the intersection of different ideas and open exploration, from tests and iterations. We've built our team and our processes to foster this kind of collaboration, all in the service of delivering success for our clients.

Research and Strategy

Our team doesn't rely on assumptions. Instead, we deep dive the context for each project - exploring market opportunities, user needs, and technical solutions. This enables us to map out a strategic development path for your product that's laser focused on de-risking and accelerating the development cycle.

See Example of Our Work

Design

Magic happens when design and engineering functions work collaboratively to create a product that is equal parts beautiful, usable, and functional. From concept ideation to model making and usability studies, our designers bring curiosity and creativity to any challenge.

See Example of Our Work

Engineering

Our electrical and mechanical engineering teams bring tenacity, perseverance, and engineering creativity to every project. This combination enables us to solve complex engineering challenges and help our clients accelerate towards manufacturing and commercialization.

See Example of Our Work

Manufacturing

We help you ship. Our team has the hard-earned experience to help our clients make the jump from prototype to manufacturable product. We offer design for manufacturability and assembly (DFM/DFA), in-house pilot production, connections to regional manufacturers, and on-site production support – everything you need to transition from 1 to 10 to 1,000 and beyond.
